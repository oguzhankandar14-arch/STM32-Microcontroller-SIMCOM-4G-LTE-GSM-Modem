
Input Voltage : 5V
Output Voltage : 3V
- LP5907MPX IC is used to generate 3V output voltage for SD Card .
- Decoupling capacitors are used to suppress the high frequency noise on the input side.
- In addition to the standard 100 nF decoupling capacitor, a 22 pF capacitor is placed in parallel to improve high-frequency noise suppression. Due to the parasitic inductance of larger capacitors, their effectiveness decreases at very high frequencies. The small-value capacitor helps to shunt high-frequency transients to ground, enhancing overall signal integrity and reducing potential EMI issues.
- To ensure a clean and stable power supply for the SD card, a ferrite bead is used to isolate high-frequency noise from the main power rail.
